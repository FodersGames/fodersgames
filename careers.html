Community Manager

Manage social media, engage with the community, and help us build our online presence.

  • Create and schedule posts

Beta Tester

Help us test games before their official release and provide feedback to improve the experience.

Job Description:

As a Beta Tester, you'll play early versions of our games, identify bugs, and provide feedback on gameplay mechanics.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Play unreleased versions of our games
  • Report bugs and glitches
  • Provide feedback on gameplay, balance, and fun factor
  • Test new features and updates

Requirements:

  • Passion for gaming
  • Attention to detail
  • Ability to give constructive feedback
